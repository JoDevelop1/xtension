# Xtension v0.4.0

- Adds **Download as PDF** to X/Twitter tweet menus.
- Exports threads by grouping contiguous posts from the same author.
- Stops thread extraction before replies published by other accounts.
- Includes quoted tweets with their available text and media.
- Adds video previews in the PDF with a note to open the tweet source for playback.
- Keeps long-form article export with structured text and images.

- `xtension-edge-v0.4.0.zip`
- `xtension-chrome-v0.4.0.zip`
- `xtension-firefox-v0.4.0.zip`
